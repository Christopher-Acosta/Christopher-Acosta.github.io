Projects

Leslie's Pool Supply Delivery Driver

I was one of 5 Averitt Express drivers chosen to serve the Leslie's account. This account was a special account where my duties were primarily to the customer itself, and not Averitt. In service of Leslie's, I pulled Leslie's trailers loaded with pool supplies including large quantities of hazardous chemicals. We had to deliver to extremely small store locations which were often challenging to get in and out of. Our trailers were equipped with lift gates and we unloaded the trailers ourselves using electric pallet jacks. A single trailer could be destined for up to 12 stores across hundreds of miles. Stores were often staffed with only one person, which forced us to emphasize coordination in terms of schedule, and I often called stores in advance, and sometimes even called managers' personal phones to coordinate after-hours or early morning deliveries. I emphasized proper notice, punctuality, respect, and deference and was often told that I was their favorite driver, and I took pride in making friends with notoriously grumpy managers.

Cracker Barrel Delivery Driver

Dispatched out of the Cracker Barrel distribution center in Lebanon, TN. This was another driving assignment where I primarily serviced a single customer, and didn't report to Averitt directly. I delivered to dozens of Cracker Barrel locations across Tennessee, Kentucky, Ohio, Indiana, Michigan, Pennsylvania, West Virginia, Georgia, Alabama, and Mississippi. I unloaded every trailer myself with a dolly and ramps. Each trailer was packed to the ceiling with hundreds of boxes and it was incredibly physically demanding work.
